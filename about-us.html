Bulelak DayCare Center was established year 1996 by Marikina Government to give quality education
for kids here in barangay Malanday. This dayCare is very child friendly place and good environment for your kids.
It has an health center just underneath it and a mini playground for childrens to play to.
There is also a chapel just a few walks away and a covered basketball court.
This good environment can surely make your kids to eagerly go to school everyday, to learn and to have fun at the same time
Our way of teaching will help your kids to improve their skills, intelligence and of course social skills at their young age
by giving them a lot of different activities.
And of course the most important thing is that we will ensure the safetyness of your children by providing good security and
good medication when needed.
